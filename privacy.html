Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

1. Who we are

ScratchDesign ("we", "us", "our") is a digital design and technology studio. Our website is scratchdesign.co. Our primary contact email is hello@scratchdesign.co.

This policy explains what information is collected when you visit our website, how it is used, and your rights in relation to it.

2. What data we collect

This website does not operate its own contact form or user accounts. We do not directly store any personal data on our servers. However, the following interactions may result in data being processed:

  • Email enquiries: If you click the email link (hello@scratchdesign.co), your email client sends a message directly to us. We retain that correspondence for the purpose of replying and managing client relationships.
  • Booking a call: When you use the "Schedule a Call" widget (powered by Cal.com), Cal.com collects your name, email address, and selected time slot on their platform. This is governed by Cal.com's Privacy Policy.

3. Third-party services

Our website loads assets and content from the following third parties. Each makes a network request that may log your IP address and browser information:

Service Purpose Data processed Privacy policy
Google Fonts Loads the Syne typeface IP address, browser UA Google Privacy
Cal.com Meeting booking widget Name, email, timezone Cal.com Privacy
Unsplash Stock photography (hero & services) IP address on image load Unsplash Privacy
WordPress.com (mshots) Live screenshots of portfolio sites IP address on image request Automattic Privacy

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other behavioural tracking or advertising technology.

4. Cookies

Our website itself does not set any first-party cookies. The Cal.com booking widget may set session cookies when you interact with it; these are governed by Cal.com's policy. Google Fonts requests do not set persistent cookies.

5. How we use your data

Any personal data we receive (e.g. via email) is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or proposal request
  • Manage an ongoing client relationship
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, our legal basis for processing personal data received via email is legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — specifically, the legitimate interest of responding to business enquiries. For bookings made via Cal.com, the legal basis is contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)).

7. Data retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to manage the relevant client relationship, and deleted when no longer needed. We do not retain any booking data ourselves — that is managed by Cal.com.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access the data we hold about you
  • Right to have inaccurate data corrected
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object to or restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@scratchdesign.co. We will respond within 30 days.

9. International transfers

Google Fonts and Cal.com may transfer data outside the EEA. Both organisations maintain Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards. See their respective privacy policies for details.

10. Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has sent us personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
