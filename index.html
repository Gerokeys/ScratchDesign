Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we start.

Can't find the answer you're looking for? We're happy to discuss your project in detail.

Talk to us
How long does a typical project take? +

A landing page typically takes 2–3 weeks. A multi-page website takes 4–8 weeks. Custom interactive experiences can take 8–16 weeks depending on complexity. We always provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project.

Do you offer branding alongside web design? +

Yes. Many of our clients come to us for complete brand identity work before the website phase begins. We offer logo design, typography systems, colour palettes, and full brand guidelines as a standalone service or bundled with your website project.

Do you work with international clients? +

Absolutely. We work with clients across Africa, Europe, the US, and beyond. Our project management is fully remote — we use Notion, Figma, and video calls to keep collaboration seamless regardless of timezone differences.

What technologies do you build with? +

We primarily work with vanilla JavaScript, GSAP, Lenis, React, and Next.js for development. For CMS, we use Sanity, Contentful, or WordPress depending on client preference. All our work is handcrafted — we never use page builders or pre-built themes.

Can you redesign our existing website? +

Yes — redesigns are one of our specialities. We conduct a thorough audit of your existing site's performance, UX, and brand alignment before proposing a strategic redesign. We focus on improving conversion, not just aesthetics.

What does the payment structure look like? +

We work on a 50% deposit to begin, 25% at design sign-off, and 25% on final delivery. For larger projects we can accommodate milestone-based payment structures. We accept bank transfers and major credit cards.
